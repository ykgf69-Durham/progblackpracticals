Kendo

What is Kendo?

Kendo is a Japanese martial art which involves striking an opponent with a sword in a sparring environment. It has many sporting aspects, but also incorporates Japanese culture and rituals into both training and competition.

What are the differences with other Japanese sword martial arts?

Iaido
  • The art of drawing and cutting with a sword in the same motion.
  • Practice with blunt metal swords.
  • Usually individual choreographed motions.
Kobudo
  • The art of cutting and finishing a target.
  • Practice with sharpened metal swords.
  • Individual choreography with a target to practice cutting cleanly.
Kendo
  • The art of swordfighting.
  • Practice with flexible bamboo swords.
  • Contact sparring with a partner in armour.
